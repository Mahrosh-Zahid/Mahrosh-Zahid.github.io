Classifier Predictions

A A A

Tools Used: R - dplyr, Rweka, partykit, caret, party, e1071, rpart, class

I conducted an experiment to explore different classifiers for predicting whether clients subscribe to a term deposit.

First, I preprocessed the dataset, dealing with unknown entries and formatting it for analysis. Then, I divided the data into training and testing sets to evaluate the performance of each classifier.

I trained the CTree, J48 Tree, Linear Classifier, and K Nearest Neighbour (k-NN) models, making predictions on the test dataset and assessing their accuracy using confusion matrices.

Finally, I compared the results of each classifier based on metrics like accuracy, error rate, precision, recall, and F1 score. Visualizing the findings with bar charts helped me understand which classifier performed best for the given dataset.
